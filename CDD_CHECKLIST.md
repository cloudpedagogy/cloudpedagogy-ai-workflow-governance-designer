# CDD_CHECKLIST.md - AI Workflow Governance Designer

## Capability Intent: Design Governed Workflows
- [ ] Explicitly define the purpose and context of the workflow.
- [ ] Map every step to a specific type (Human, AI, Decision).
- [ ] Identify inputs and outputs for every workflow stage.

## Capability Intent: Enforce Accountability
- [ ] Assign a responsible role for every step.
- [ ] Define intervention points for human oversight.
- [ ] Explicitly mark decision points requiring a Decision Record.

## Capability Intent: Expose AI Boundaries
- [ ] Categorize AI involvement (None, Support, Generated).
- [ ] Visualize AI dependency across the entire workflow.
- [ ] Highlight steps where AI is high-risk and lacks oversight.

## Capability Intent: Risk Awareness & Mitigation
- [ ] Identify Bias, Operational, and Ethical risks per step.
- [ ] Define specific mitigation strategies for identified risks.
- [ ] Calculate a Governance Completeness Index based on captured data.

## Capability Intent: Reviewability & Traceability
- [ ] Provide a structured reflection layer for post-design analysis.
- [ ] Enable export to immutable formats (PDF, Markdown).
- [ ] Maintain a versioned schema for exportable data.
