# CDD_EVIDENCE.md - AI Workflow Governance Designer

## Evidence of Capability-Driven Development

### 1. Traceability
- **Metric**: Governance Completeness Index.
- **Evidence**: The UI explicitly tracks which fields are missing and how complete the governance record is.
- **Location**: Dashboard / Export Section.

### 2. Accountability
- **Metric**: Responsibility Allocation.
- **Evidence**: Every workflow step requires an assigned "Responsible Role" and "Human Oversight Level".
- **Location**: Step Builder -> Human-AI Boundary.

### 3. Risk Awareness
- **Metric**: Risk Layer Integration.
- **Evidence**: Risks (Bias, Ethics, Privacy) are first-class citizens in the step editor, not optional notes.
- **Location**: Step Builder -> Risk Identification Layer.

### 4. Reviewability
- **Metric**: Governance-Ready Export.
- **Evidence**: Support for Markdown and PDF exports that present the workflow in a structured, audit-ready format.
- **Location**: Export Section.

### 5. AI Boundary Definition
- **Metric**: AI Dependency Score.
- **Evidence**: Visual indicator showing the concentration of AI reliance across the workflow.
- **Location**: Reflection Layer / AI Dependency Map.
